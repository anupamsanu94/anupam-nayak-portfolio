---
title: Case Study -> Designing Scalable Connector Documentation
---

## 🔹 Problem

Connector documentation was inconsistent across multiple data sources, making it difficult for users to configure and optimize integrations.

---

## 🔹 Approach

- Collaborated with SMEs and product teams  
- Standardized documentation structure across connectors  
- Introduced reusable templates  

---

## 🔹 Solution

Created a consistent documentation model:

1. Overview  
2. Prerequisites  
3. Configuration Steps  
4. Use Cases  
5. Performance Tuning  
6. FAQs & Troubleshooting  

---

## 🔹 Impact

- Improved discoverability and usability  
- Reduced support queries  
- Enabled faster onboarding for users
